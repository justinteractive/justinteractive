Philosophy

We believe that active management driven by bottom-up, fundamental research can uncover and exploit inefficiencies in equity markets. We maintain a collaborative environment that encourages interconnected perspectives, which we believe enhances our ability to generate competitive returns for our clients. Our portfolio managers focus on risk versus reward and will accept risks when their valuation analysis indicates that such risks are in line with a client's risk/return expectations.

Process

Our investment process is focused on proprietary, fundamental research with an emphasis on the integration of diverse perspectives into well-informed security selection. Security selection is primarily responsible for alpha generation, while effective communication, collaboration, and transparency throughout the entire equity and fixed income investment platforms bolster all aspects of our process. In the vast majority of cases, a single decision maker is ultimately responsible for a strategy, allowing investment decisions to be based on consistent stock comparisons and the ability to move nimbly when it is advantageous to do so. Team-based resources often assist the lead decision maker.
