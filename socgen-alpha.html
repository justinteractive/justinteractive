Socgen Alpha FX

Working with Lab49 I spent a three month period as the lead of the UX team on their SDP Alpha FX. Working closely with stakeholders, business analysts, developers and visual designers in an agile team. I took business direction from the BA's and then worked closely with them to rapidly sketch out potential solutions to their requirements. We spent some time on the trading floor when we really needed user insight and then worked through our proposed solutions with the lead Microsoft dev team so they could build the fixtures and classes while I worked with our visual design team to deliver the components using Blend and Illustrator.
