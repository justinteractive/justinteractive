About me

I’m a digital product and service designer with over 20 years of experience leading design, UX, and innovation across global agencies, fintech, and startups.

I've worked with dozens of design agencies, helping two open their first offices in London, then spent 15 years shaping the next generation of global trading platforms for major investment banks.

I’ve taken three startups from 0–1, most recently my own sustainability startup, growing it to £1M in three years before exiting in 2024.

I turn my hand to everything to get things moving and seek out brilliant people as I hand over.

I’m a hands-on sleeves rolled up leader and problem solver, obsessed with craft, clarity, and progress.

I bridge design, business, product, and engineering - ensuring we meet expectations and deliver work that’s simple, elegant, and world-class.

I believe high standards aren’t optional - they’re the difference between the 1% and the rest.

From strategy to execution. Let’s play.
