RBS Risk management

An exploratory piece using a handful of credit officers at RBS as my users and SME's, I worked alone exploring the opportunities to produce small self contained WPF components that could deliver an overview and detail drill down into the entire risk portfolio by book, trader, desk, team etc.

This simple widget set the course for a larger credit portal project that I subsequently spent 6 months working on at RBS Bankside in 2012. I cannot show that work publicly as the project is still being built, however I can bring examples to a meeting.
